About PURPLE 2024

I am excited to share information about PURPLE 2024, the annual children's charity program hosted by Ndifreke Andrew-Essien, the founder of FAECARE Foundation. This event is dedicated to supporting children from diverse backgrounds, including orphans, vulnerable children, the less privileged, children with disabilities, and many more.
